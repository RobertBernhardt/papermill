Potentially Asked Questions

Because we know you're wondering but are too afraid to ask

Is this ethical?

+

That's a philosophical question, isn't it? Our service is designed as a research and learning tool. We provide papers that can serve as advanced study guides, reference materials, or inspiration for your own work. Using our papers as your own submission without proper attribution would be academically dishonest, which we absolutely do not encourage. But we can't control what you do with our content once it's in your hands, just like a knife manufacturer can't control whether you use their product to spread butter or commit crimes against haute cuisine

Will my paper pass AI detection?

+

Yes, our papers are specifically engineered to bypass common AI detection systems. We use a sophisticated process that produces text indistinguishable from human writing. That said, we continuously update our methods as detection technology evolves. For additional security, we recommend making minor edits to further personalize the content

How long does it take to generate a paper?

+

Basic papers are typically ready within 30 minutes. More complex work like Master's or PhD level content may take 1-2 hours. If you select our "Hardcore" quality control option, add another hour as our system performs extensive verification and refinement

What if I'm not satisfied with my paper?

+

We offer one free revision within 24 hours of delivery. If you're still unhappy after the revision, we provide a 70% refund. We retain 30% to cover our operational costs. However, our satisfaction rate is over 98%, so this rarely happens

Do I own the content that's generated?

+

Yes, once delivered, the content belongs to you. You can use it as you see fit—reference material, learning aid, or... other academic purposes. Our system generates unique content for each order, ensuring you receive original material that won't be provided to anyone else

Can I get caught using this service?

+

We take extensive precautions to ensure your privacy and anonymity. We don't store your papers after delivery, we use secure payment processing, and we never share your information. That said, we recommend using the papers as reference rather than submitting them directly—partly for ethical reasons, but also because professors may recognize when a student suddenly writes at a different level than usual
